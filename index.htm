Our Core Services

Customs Clearing (CHA)

Fast, reliable, and fully compliant Customs Clearing Services that simplify complex import-export formalities. Includes Import & Export Clearance, DGFT Licensing, and documentation handling.

Sea Freight Handling

Cost-effective and efficient sea freight management with strong carrier networks. We handle FCL, LCL, port formalities, and customs clearance, ensuring smooth global cargo movement with full visibility.

Air Freight Handling

Fast, reliable, and secure air cargo solutions for time-critical shipments. End-to-end coordination across airlines, terminals, and customs for quick transit and accurate documentation.

Transportation & Logistics

Comprehensive cargo transport (Air/Sea/Road) and Door-to-Door Logistics Support, managing freight coordination from origin terminal to final destination.

Warehousing & SCM

Integrated Warehousing and end-to-end supply chain solutions to optimize inventory management, reduce costs, and improve logistics efficiency.

Port / CFS Coordination

Expert handling and coordination with port authorities and Container Freight Stations (CFS) to ensure smooth cargo movement and minimized dwell time.
